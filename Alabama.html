Birmingham County

AIDS Alabama

 Offers mental health services, substance abuse help, free HIV and STI testing, PrEP, and PEP. Provides permanent housing, rapid rehousing and youth housing (for ages 19 to 24) for individuals no matter their HIV status; and a shelter and rental assistance for individuals that are HIV positive.

Magic City Wellness Center

 Provides primary care, PrEP, Hormone Replacement Therapy, STD/STI testing and treatment, counseling, and support groups.
