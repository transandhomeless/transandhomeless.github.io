Alaska

If you make less than $22,597/year before taxes and deductions, consider applying for Medicaid. It will cover all trans healthcare, including HRT and surgeries. It will also cover other types of healthcare, like primary care and mental health services.

If you have a bank balance of $2,001 or less and make less than $22,087/year before taxes, consider applying for SNAP benefits (money for food)

If you earn less than $300/month and you have an immediate and specific need for shelter (eviction notice), utilities (shut-off notice), food, clothing, or burial; consider applying for General Relief (cash given out monthly).
