Connecticut

If you make less than $1,563/month consider applying for HUSKY Health. It will completely cover transgender healthcare, including therapy, HRT, and transgender surgeries.

If you are low income, consider applying for SNAP benefits (money for food).

Fairfield County

Triangle Community Center

Offers mental health services, support groups, case management, and help with substance abuse.

Kids in Crisis

Provides temporary shleter to youth up to age 17. Provides counseling, medical assessment, educational support and group counseling.

Hartford County

Prudence Crandal Center

Provides emergency shelter, transitional housing and permanent housing to victims of domestic violence. Has free individual counseling and support groups.

Litchfield County

Safe Haven

Offers emergencey shelter and rapid rehousing to victims of domestic violence.

Middlesex County

Middlesex Health

Helps connect with providers and services, including: specially-trained primary care providers, gender affirming hormone therapy, and gender affirming surgery. Has support groups.

Has support groups, case management, a food pantry and a clothing closet. Has a scholarship fund for youth.
