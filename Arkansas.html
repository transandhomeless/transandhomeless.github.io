Arkansas

Pulaski County

Lucie's Place

Provides long-term housing for youth ages 18 to 25. Has a drop-in center for people of all ages. Services provided at the drop-in center include case management, referrals to other programs in the area, taking a shower, wash laundry, have a meal, get gender-affirming clothes from the closet, start therapy or the process of changing your legal name.

Sebastian County

Jessi's House

Provides transitional housing for young adults ages 18 to 25.

Washington County

Vector

Offers primary care services, sexual and reproductive health (including birth control and STI testing); and HRT for patients 15 and older.

Peace at Home

Provides survivors of domestic violence with emergency shelter, financial assistance, transportation, and counseling.

Northwest Arkansas Equality

Provides free HIV/STI testing services, various support groups and resources.
