California

Consider applying to Medi-Cal if one of these apply to you:

-You earn less than $18,755 before taxes and other deductions

-You are 65 or older

-You are blind or disabled

-You are under the age of 21

-You are pregnant

Why apply to Medi-Cal? It will 100% cover all of your medical visits, including primary care visits, mental health, any bloodwork, HRT, and all trans surgeries.

If you have a bank balance of $2,001 or less and make less than $17,667/year before taxes, consider applying for SNAP benefits (money for food)

Lastly, if you have no income, you can apply to General Relief (this is cash you will receive each month). To apply for General Relief, you should look up "general relief [county you live in]"
